01   AboutThe short version

half engineer, half artist

I architect generative 3D pipelines at Meta Reality Labs — characters, animation, and environments for Horizon. I'm the first inventor on the patent for the USD-based avatar pipeline that ships to billions of users across Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp.

Before Meta I spent a decade at Disney Animation and DreamWorks simulating hair, cloth, and flesh on Encanto, Zootopia, Moana, Frozen, and others — leading 40-person tech-anim teams and building the studio tooling underneath.

The work I care about lives in the seam: where a training run, a USD composition, a director's note, and a deadline have to become the same thing. I build the infrastructure other artists never have to think about — scriptable solvers, parametric character systems, node-graph asset pipelines, Gaussian splat character — and the cloud plumbing underneath.

04   Experience20 years · 3 chapters

a career in simulation, systems, scale

Technical Art Lead Meta · Reality Labs 2022 — Present

Technical art lead for generative AI 3D pipelines — characters, animation, and environments for Horizon. I prototype the dataset, define the roadmap with XFN partners, staff the team, build the demo, and stay with the model from training through ship. The job spans research collaboration, pipeline architecture, cloud orchestration, and the long tail of making generative output actually shippable.

  • Architectural lead and first inventor of the patented avatar content pipeline — a dynamic parametric system built on OpenUSD, delivering personalized characters to billions of users across the Family of Apps.
  • Designed and shipped pipelines for Gaussian splat characters alongside traditional polygon characters — unified asset graph, shared variant logic, common authoring tools.
  • Led backend design of Artcastle, a USD-based cloud content store tracking dependencies across Meta Avatars, Fantastical Avatars, Codec Avatars, and Instagram Pets.
  • Built the parametric character system that lets one base mesh fan out into millions of personalized identities — facial geometry, hair, body shape, garment fit — each composed from a graph of USD layers.
  • Partnered with research to productionize generative models for character creation, asset generation, and animation synthesis — owning the path from notebook to deployed inference.
  • Driving the open-sourcing of Noodles (Meta's high-performance node-graph graphics library) and usdNoodles — an upcoming addition to Pixar's OpenUSD.
  • Deployed C++ across the Family of Apps; staffed and grew the tech-art organization underneath.
